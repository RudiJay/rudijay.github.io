Keep Games Interesting

Hi, I'm Rudi, and I'm a game developer. I currently specialise in programming but wear all sorts of other hats like designer, 2D artist, and producer to get games made.

I am currently working for Loaded Games, the games development studio I founded alongside two of my closest friends in the seaside town of Plymouth, England. As a studio, we are working towards on our first release, "Menace!". Previously I was part of the programming team at Supermassive Games, where I mainly worked on gameplay programming, prototyping, virtual reality, and UI. While there I helped ship two triple-A virtual reality titles, "The Inpatient" and "Bravo Team" for PS4.

I'm enthusiastic about games, both as a medium and an industry, and committed to keeping them both as interesting and exciting as possible. Whether I'm as interesting is up for debate. I love drawing, music, cult cinema, and all sorts of stupid other stuff.

I also study at the University of Plymouth, on the final year of the Computing & Games Development course.
